Cricket Sport
Cricket is a popular sport played in many countries around the world. It is a team sport played with a bat and a ball, with each team consisting of 11 players. The objective of the game is to score more runs than the opposing team.

Cricket has a rich history and is steeped in tradition, with many countries having their own unique styles of play and rivalries. It is a highly popular sport in countries such as India, Pakistan, Australia, England, South Africa, and the West Indies, among others.
